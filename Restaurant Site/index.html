About Elysium

Elysium is a locally-owned, locally-sourced restaurant located in New Haven, Connecticut.

We specialize in comfort food, with an emphasis on vegetarian and vegan options.

We offer lunch and dinner menus, with weekly rotating options, as well as daily specials.

Our tables are available to reserve every night of the week.
